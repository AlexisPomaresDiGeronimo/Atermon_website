Our Vision

Managed Staffing, Inc. is one of the top award-winning full-service diversity MSP providers, understanding not only the MSP environment but the software, the SLAs and the commitment it takes to make each program a success. Managed Staffing account managers and recruiters have over 15 years of experience necessary to effectively provide to our clients and deliver well-qualified professionals efficiently and expeditiously. Through consulting and understanding our clients’ needs, Managed Staffing is empowered to streamline the staffing augmentation process—allowing for faster response and more affordable services. Our client-dedicated account managers and support team empower Managed Staffing to not only provide services, but team up with our clients to give real-time market analysis and feedback, and build a business strategy that will help with: project planning, contingent workforce outlook, and budget forecasting, reduce onboard time, and decrease costs. This allows a win-win situation for everyone. No matter how large or complex your project may be, we will assess your contingent workforce needs and support your vision to ensure projects are completed on time and on budget. We have a team of over 120 dedicated recruiters that focus on project-based staffing, rapid deployment projects, temporary workforce solutions, permanent placement, and temp-to-perm needs to ensure business continuity. Their expertise and resources deliver quality professionals who are among the most skilled and diverse in their industry.
